Maira Riga

Research Assistant

I am a fourth-year student in Psychology at the University of Aberdeen. I am interested in everything brain-related, visual neuroscience in particular. I joined the Manassi Lab in 2020, and I am working on developing an illusion of stability through an active mechanism of serial dependence.
